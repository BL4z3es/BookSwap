Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BookSwap

Click on 'List a Book' in the navigation menu. Fill in the book details including title, subject, grade, and condition. Add a photo if possible, choose your preferred contact method, and publish. Your listing will be live immediately!
On any book listing page, you'll see the owner's preferred contact method. Click the 'Contact Owner' button to reach out via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Be polite, introduce yourself, and arrange a convenient time and place to meet.
Yes, absolutely! BookSwap is 100% free for everyone. We don't charge any fees for listing books, contacting owners, or using any features of the platform. Our goal is to help families share educational resources, not to make money.
Only your display name and general location (city/area) are visible on your listings. Your email, phone number, or other contact details are never publicly displayed. When someone wants to contact you, they do so through the contact method you've chosen.
We recommend meeting in public places during daylight hours—school parking lots, shopping centers, or coffee shops work great. Consider bringing a friend or family member. Trust your instincts, and if something feels off, it's okay to cancel.
Book owners can mark their listings as 'Reserved' if someone has expressed interest. This helps prevent multiple people from showing up for the same book. If you're interested in a book, reach out to the owner quickly!
We ask all listers to honestly describe their book's condition. If you receive a book that's significantly worse than described, please contact the owner directly. If there's a serious issue, you can report the listing to our support team.
You can delete your account in your Dashboard under Settings. This will remove all your listings and personal information from our platform. This action cannot be undone.
