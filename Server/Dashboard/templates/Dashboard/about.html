About BookSwap

We believe every child deserves access to quality education materials, without the financial burden that often comes with it.

Our Mission

BookSwap was created to solve a simple problem: perfectly good textbooks sitting unused on shelves while other families struggle to afford new ones. We're building a community where sharing is the norm, not the exception.

Community First

We're not a business—we're a community of parents helping parents.

Sustainability

Every book reused is one less book in a landfill.

Accessibility

Education shouldn't be limited by what families can afford.

Why We Built This

It started with a simple frustration: endless WhatsApp group messages at the start of every school year. "Does anyone have last year's Maths book?" "Looking for Grade 10 English textbook!" Messages getting lost, no organization, and parents spending hours trying to coordinate.

We realized there had to be a better way. A central place where families could list their books, browse what's available, and connect directly with each other—without the chaos of group chats.

BookSwap is completely free because we believe access to education materials shouldn't have a price tag. No fees, no commissions, just a community platform built to make sharing easier.

Your Privacy Matters

We only collect the information necessary to help you list and find books. Your personal details are never shared without your consent, and we never sell data.
